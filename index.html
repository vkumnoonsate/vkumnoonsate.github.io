OXFAM UI/UX CASE STUDY

Oxfam is a global organization,
which works with over
90 countries to end poverty worldwide.

The goal was to redesign the current Oxfam
website by executing the user experience (UX)
and user interface (UI) design process to
emulate better navigation, aesthetics,
and the overall purpose Oxfam stands for.

UC RIVERSIDE R'GEAR 2019

Every year, UC Riverside provides free
sweaters for the students to maximize
school pride and also give apparel for those
who may not be able to afford it. The goal
was to create a design that encompasses
UC Riverside'skey features of the campus such
as the bell tower and the 'C'.

This design was created in collaboration
with the Associated Students of
UC Riverside's marketing team.
